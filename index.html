FAQ

Quick answers for the questions members usually ask first.

These answers cover the most common starting points for new and returning members.

What is Study With Me?
Study With Me is a global Discord server centered around focused study, accountability, and a student-friendly support system. Members use the space to study together, stay consistent, find resources, and participate in a community that understands academic pressure.
Why does SWM use member verification?
Verification helps protect the server and maintain a safer environment. Strix handles the process and removes users who do not complete verification within 48 hours, helping the community stay secure and intentional.
What are the main study tools inside the server?
SWM uses native tools like plantwithme, studywithme, and Strix, along with accountability groups for apps such as Forest and YPT. These systems help members build habits, track participation, and stay engaged.
Are there spaces for different kinds of students?
Yes. The server includes a variety of channels for general studying, focus sessions, resources, community support, and future course-specific spaces so members can find the environment that fits them best.
Does SWM offer anything beyond study rooms?
Absolutely. Members can access digital library spaces, use stickers and emojis, join recognition systems like weekly and monthly leaderboards, and participate in a broader student community culture.
How do leaderboards and recognition work?
SWM highlights members through weekly leaderboards, the Hall of Legends, Hall of Class I Users, and voting perks. These systems are designed to recognize consistency, effort, and contribution to the community.
Can I find course-specific channels now?
That part is planned next. The website already includes a placeholder for a future “See Available Specific Course Channels” button so we can add the feature cleanly when you are ready.
Will more detailed guides be added here?
Yes. This site is designed to grow with the server, so more detailed references, guides, and resource sections can be added over time as the community expands.

Student Handbook

Rules should feel clear, fair, and easy to understand at a glance.

This section frames the handbook as polished website content rather than a long wall of text, making the rules easier to scan and understand.

Respect the learning space

Members should help protect a calm, supportive environment where people can focus without harassment, hostility, or disruption.

  • Respect other members and moderators
  • Keep interactions constructive and appropriate for a student community
  • Do not disrupt shared study spaces

Use access responsibly

Verification, account safety, and honest participation help keep SWM trustworthy and useful.

  • Complete verification within the required timeframe
  • Do not misuse bots, automated systems, or privileged roles
  • Protect your account and server access

Contribute with care

Resources, channel use, and public participation should make the server better for everyone, not noisier or harder to manage.

  • Share useful resources responsibly
  • Stay on topic in specialized spaces
  • Follow moderator direction and handbook updates
